Market Insight

Rubber components are indispensable across various sectors, with industrial rubber sales soaring to US$ 54.2 billion in 2021. Despite facing challenges during the COVID-19 pandemic, the market has shown resilience and is projected to grow at a CAGR of 1.3% from 2024 to 2032. The automotive tyre sector alone consumes 50% of the rubber produced globally, highlighting the critical role of rubber in our daily lives.

Sourcing premium rubber sheets and scrap to delivering top-quality products for the automotive, footwear, and industrial sectors, our expertise ensures resilient and profitable solutions for your business.

What we offer

At Perfect Trade Links, we are dedicated to bridging the gap between rubber producers and various industries, offering unparalleled solutions in the rubber processing and manufacturing sectors. Recognized as a key player in the natural rubber market, we understand the profound impact of rubber in the global economy and strive to harness its potential to meet the growing demands across multiple industries. Our vision is to become the foremost authority in rubber trading by providing high-quality rubber sheets and scrap from farmers, thereby empowering the agricultural community while ensuring a steady supply of essential raw materials to industries like manufacturing, mining, and energy.

Market Insight

Rubber components are indispensable across various sectors, with industrial rubber sales soaring to US$ 54.2 billion in 2021. Despite facing challenges during the COVID-19 pandemic, the market has shown resilience and is projected to grow at a CAGR of 1.3% from 2024 to 2032. The automotive tyre sector alone consumes 50% of the rubber produced globally, highlighting the critical role of rubber in our daily lives.

Eswary Herbals

Premium cosmetic brand with a focus on 100% organic ingredients, offering the highest-quality hair oil, eyeliner, serums, face wash, and more. Committed to purity and effectiveness, sets a new standard in natural beauty.

What we offer

Founded in 2019 and revitalized in 2023 with an investment from Tanzero Enterprises, Eswary Herbal is an emerging force in the cosmetic industry, primarily based in Kerala. Known for its unwavering commitment to purity and quality, the brand sets a new benchmark for organic cosmetic products. With meticulous research and innovation, Eswary Herbal has surpassed all microbiological tests, ensuring that each product embodies the essence of natural beauty and safety. As a pre-revenue business, Eswary Herbal is diligently finalizing the last stages of product testing to make its highly-anticipated market debut.

Our Services

The brand’s flagship offering, the Eswary Herbal Hair Oil, is a testament to its dedication to excellence. Crafted with 100% organic ingredients, this hair oil boasts the highest quality standards, making it a superior choice for consumers seeking natural and effective hair care solutions. Beyond hair oil, Eswary Herbal offers a diverse range of products, including eyeliner, hair serum, face wash, and various other cosmetic essentials. Each product reflects the brand’s ethos of harnessing the power of nature to promote wellness and beauty.

Why Choose Eswary Herbal?

As Eswary Herbal prepares to launch under the stewardship of Tanzero Enterprises, it stands poised to make a significant impact on the organic cosmetics landscape. This partnership represents a fusion of expertise and passion for delivering high-quality, eco-friendly products that resonate with today’s conscientious consumers. Embrace the future of beauty with Eswary Herbal—where nature meets innovation.
